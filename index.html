Hobbies & Interests

Traveling is one of my greatest passions. From exploring the vibrant streets of Jaipur to soaking in the peaceful landscapes of Mussoorie, Dehradun, and Nainital, every trip helps me gain new perspectives on life. Each destination teaches me something valuable, whether it's the people, culture, or simple beauty of nature.

Philosophy has always intrigued me. The teachings of Osho, Kabir, and Rumi guide me in my everyday life. Osho’s reminder to “Be — don’t try to become” keeps me grounded, while Rumi’s wisdom, “Let yourself be drawn by the strange pull of what you really love,” encourages me to follow my passions without fear. Philosophy helps me stay centered and aligned with my true self.

I’m also passionate about planting trees. There’s something deeply fulfilling about nurturing nature and contributing to the environment. It reminds me of the importance of patience and growth, both in nature and in life.

Gaming, especially GTA 5 and Red Dead Redemption 2, is a way for me to unwind. The characters, like Arthur Morgan, teach me about integrity, making tough choices, and finding meaning in everything I do. These games, much like life, show that every decision shapes our journey.

Lastly, nature provides the balance I need in life. Whether it’s the sound of birds chirping or the sight of a clear sky, I find peace and inspiration in the outdoors.
